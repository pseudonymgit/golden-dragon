Where Family
Gathers

For over four decades, our dining room has been a backdrop for first dates, anniversaries, graduations, and weekly family dinners. We don't just serve food; we serve memories.

Our kitchen remains rooted in the traditional techniques brought over by our founder, Master Chef Lin. From hand-folded dumplings to wok-hei infused fried rice, every dish is prepared with intention and care.

House Specialties

Dishes that have stood the test of time, beloved by our regulars.

Imperial Peking Duck

Crispy skin, tender meat, served with handmade scallion pancakes, cucumber, and house plum sauce.

Fiery Szechuan Beef

Wok-seared flank steak with dried chilies, Szechuan peppercorns, and fresh vegetables in a spicy garlic sauce.

Golden Crispy Egg Rolls

A timeless classic. Hand-rolled daily with shredded pork, cabbage, and celery, fried to golden perfection.
